Thank You, Caregivers!

Celebrating
Family Caregiver’s Month

November is National Family Caregiver’s Month and Cisco and CircleOf have teamed up to support you!

We know that caregiving can be tough, and we want to support you.

What is
Circle Of?

CircleOf is a digital caregiving app that helps caregivers and patients organize and mobilize their family, friends and co-workers into on-demand care communities. CircleOf simplifies the logistics of care by making it easy to privately share information, provide assistance, and volunteer knowledge and time.

Highlighted Features

Build Care Communities Fast

Invite and connect with family, friends and co-workers.

Coordinate Care in One Place

Organize your rides, meals, child-care, home help, doctor appointments and more.

Connect to Employer Resources

Find employer benefits that can help you today.

Collaborate Securely and Privately

Communicate with your team on a private and secure platform.
